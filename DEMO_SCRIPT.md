## Exact Narration & Choreography Script

### Scene 1: The Problem & Vision (0:00 – 0:10 | 10 seconds)
* **Visual**: Open browser at `http://localhost:5173/`. Clean light Apple Enterprise Dashboard is visible.
* **Choreography**: Glide cursor gently across the top executive bar showing **Good Morning / InventoryPulse AI**, then pause slightly over the **$378,046 Revenue at Risk** card.
* **Exact Verbatim Narration**:
  > *"Traditional inventory systems tell you what happened. InventoryPulse AI tells you what will happen next—and automatically recommends actions before problems occur."*

---

### Scene 2: AI War Room & Wow Moment #1 (0:10 – 0:35 | 25 seconds)
* **Visual**: Click **Operations Center (`/war-room`)** in the left Apple navigation sidebar.
* **Choreography**:
  1. Hover smoothly across the top KPI cards: **$378,046 Revenue at Risk**, **6 Potential Stockouts**, **3 Critical Suppliers**, and **94.8% AI Consensus Confidence**.
  2. Move cursor down to the first Critical Risk Card: **Industrial Sensor X200 (ELEC-SEN-200)**.
  3. Highlight the red badge: **4d to Stockout** (`$18,400 Exposure`).
  4. **WOW MOMENT #1**: Click the blue **[Approve PO]** / **[Execute AI PO]** button on the Sensor X200 card to trigger automated EDI procurement.
* **Exact Verbatim Narration**:
  > *"Our AI continuously monitors inventory, supplier performance, and operational risks. It identifies issues before they impact revenue.*  
  > *The system predicts this product will stock out in four days. Revenue impact: eighteen thousand four hundred dollars. The AI already recommends an autonomous replenishment action."*

### Scene 3: Executive Copilot & Wow Moment #2 (0:35 – 1:10 | 35 seconds)
* **Visual**: Click **Executive Copilot (`/copilot`)** in the sidebar.
* **Choreography**:
  1. Click the first prompt suggestion chip or type: `"Why did inventory costs increase this month?"`.
  2. Point cursor to the structured 3-part Executive Brief:
     * **1. Diagnostic Root Cause**
     * **2. Quantified Business Impact**
     * **3. Recommended Autonomous Action Plan** + **99.4% Confidence Gauge**.
  3. **WOW MOMENT #2**: Click the query chip: `"What should I reorder today?"` and point to the instant auto-generated Purchase Order recommendation card.
* **Exact Verbatim Narration**:
  > *"Instead of static dashboards, executives can ask questions directly. The AI identifies root causes, business impact, risk levels, and recommended actions.*  
  > *When we ask what to reorder today, the system not only analyzes data—it recommends concrete, executable business decisions."*

### Scene 4: Supply Chain Digital Twin & Wow Moment #3 (1:10 – 1:40 | 30 seconds)
* **Visual**: Click **Digital Twin Sim (`/simulation`)** in the sidebar.
* **Choreography**:

  3. **WOW MOMENT #3**: Click and drag the **Supplier SLA Delay** slider smoothly from `4 Days` up to **20 Days**.
  4. Point as the metrics recalculate instantly in real time: **Revenue Delta**, **Projected Stockouts (7 SKUs)**, **Composite Risk Score**, and the updated **AI Recommended Safeguards**.
* **Exact Verbatim Narration**:
  > *"InventoryPulse AI includes a supply-chain digital twin. Teams can simulate future scenarios before making decisions.*  
  > *In seconds, leaders can understand the impact of severe supplier disruptions—and proactively deploy automated safety stock buffers."*
